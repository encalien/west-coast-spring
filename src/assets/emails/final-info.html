Final Information

Only a few days left until Winter Westie Weekend. In this email you'll find some important information regarding the coming event. Please read them carefully, and if you have any questions, don't hesitate to ask on Facebook event or via email.

Check in

Check-in will be possible at the party on Friday night between 19:30 and 24.00 and on Saturday before workshops between 11.00 and 12.00. We only need your name for the check-in. If you haven't sorted out your payment yet, you can also do that in cash upon arrival. If you already paid, but have not received a payment confirmation email, please bring proof of payment with you.

Please note: If you have a FULL PASS, then all party entrance fees are already included. They will have a guest list at the door - state your name and that you're there for west coast swing, and they will let you through.

If you only have a Saturday pass, or no pass, you will need to pay the entrance fee to Studio Dansa at the door, before you can check in to the event in the ballroom itself.

Location

The entire weekend program will happen in the dance school Studio Dansa, located on Vilharjeva 3, 1000 Ljubljana.

You can park on multiple paid parking lots in the area, the closest one is on Vilharjeva ulica, a little bit further down the street. Parking is also possible on the public parking spaces next to the streets if they are marked with white line. On those spaces parking is usually free over weekends, except Saturday mornings. There are parking machines situated on the streets where you can check the free vs. paid hours.

Schedule

The current schedule is available also as an online calendar. You can add it directly through Google calendars or download the ics file, so you can import it into an app of your choice. If the schedule changes, we will update the online calendar first and post them on Facebook event as well.


Health and security

We're planning to comply with any health recommendations that may be in place at the time of the event. We kindly ask that you do not attend the event in case you're showing symptoms of infectious disease or have tested positive for the new coronavirus. We will also provide sanitizers, which we encourage you to use regularly.

Please take care of your personal belongings and don't leave them unattended, we take no responsibility for any lost or stolen items.

You participate in the event at your own risk.

Please find here more information about the Event rules.

Please bring your own water bottle and clean dance or indoor shoes.

We're looking forward to welcoming you at our event!
